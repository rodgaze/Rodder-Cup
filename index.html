Round 2 — Best Ball · All Groups Start Simultaneously
White tees. Two-man teams, match play. REVERSE SHOTGUN — all groups start simultaneously from different holes at 7:30am sharp. Best (lowest) net score between partners is carded. Lowest net individual score between teams wins the hole. Only 5.5 hrs until Round 3 — no butterfly chasing. Pick up if you're out of the hole!
🏌️ Best Ball Format
• Each player plays their own ball the entire hole
• The best (lowest) net score between partners is carded
• Team with the lowest net individual score wins the hole

Round 3 — Greensomes · Straight Tee Times
White tees. Two-man teams, match play. Both players drive, best ball selected, non-selected player hits next, then alternate until carded.
🔄 Greensomes Format
• Both partners hit a tee shot
• Select the best drive
• The player whose drive was not selected plays the next shot
• Partners then alternate shots until the ball is holed
• Lowest net score between teams wins the hole

⛳ Rodder Cup Rules 2026
🏌️
General Rules
Applies to all rounds
  • All rounds played from the White Tees. You may play further back by mutual agreement with your opponent.
  • No player gets more than 1 stroke on any Par 3.
  • No carryovers in any Rodder Cup format.
  • No "give me" putts — unless someone says "it's good," you're putting it out.
🌿
The Savannah Rule ⭐
Lost ball procedure — know this cold
All lost balls are played under NCGA Rule 26.1C — "Lateral Water Hazard" (even where marked with white stakes).

How it works:
  • Drop a ball within two club lengths of where the ball entered the hazard
  • No closer to the hole than where it entered
  • Drop from where the ball entered — not where it landed
  • One stroke penalty assessed
  • Applies everywhere, including white-staked areas
